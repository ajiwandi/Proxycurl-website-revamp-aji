Proxycurl is a Clearbit alternative

Clearbit is a clear winner for marketers and sales reps. Proxycurl has the equivalent featureset, but for software teams to implement Clearbit features within their internal tooling without the Enterprise lock-in that Clearbit requires.

Let's talk about the solutions Clearbit offer, and how you can replicate that with Proxycurl API. We are going to go through point by point as to how you can replicate that with Proxycurl.

Prospecting

Data-driven prospecting

Clearbit defines data-driven prospecting as understanding who is visiting your website, and building your own exhaustive list from what you perceive as an "ideal customer profile".

With Proxycurl's Reveal API, you get the equivalent of Clearbit Reveal.

On top of that, you can build an exhaustive list of prospects by filtering LinkDB based on your constraints.

Enhanced & Preceise

Enhanced Ad Performance and Preceise B2B Ad Targeting

This solution is about how you can build a prospect list and then retarget these LinkedIn prospects on Facebook products.

You can do the same with Proxycurl by:

Using our Personal Email Lookup Endpoint, correspond each LinkedIn profile with a personal email via our Personal Email Lookup API Endpoint.

Building a custom audience on Facebook Ad Manager

Launch ad campaigns with the custom audience group that you had just created

Campaigns

Tailored Email Campaigns

This solution is about how you can build a prospect list and then target these LinkedIn prospects via cold emails.

You can do the same with Proxycurl by using our Personal Email Lookup Endpoint, correspond each LinkedIn profile with a verified work email via our Work Email Lookup API Endpoint.

Web Content

Dynamic Web Content

Our Reveal API will tell you which employees of which company is visiting your website.

Sales Alerts

Instant Sales Alerts

Same thing, with Reveal API, you can find out which company is interested in your product because he visited your website.

Within the API result, you can get decision maker, profile data, and contact email, so you can alert your team for an instant action on the intent.

Profile Enrichment

Enrichment

Proxycurl API is a pay-as-you-go profile enrichment API, like Clearbit, but far richer with personal emails, personal numbers, work emails, inferred salary, etc for people. For companies, we have their LinkedIn profile data, and their funding data.
